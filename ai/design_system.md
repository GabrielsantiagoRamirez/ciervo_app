# Ciervo Design System

## Colors
- Primary: premium gold `#D8B45F`
- Primary high: `#F0D58A`
- Primary dark: `#B88932`
- Secondary: deep emerald `#0F3D35`
- Secondary high: `#155C4C`
- Secondary soft: `#A7C8B8`
- Night background: `#070708`
- Night surfaces: `#151619`, `#24262B`
- Day background: `#F8F4EA`
- Day surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Day surface high: `#E8DFC8`

## State Colors
- Error: `#E25D5D`
- Success: `#2EAD74`
- Warning: `#D89B3D`
- Info: `#5A8DEE`

## Product Experience Modes
- Day means daytime businesses, plans, events, and promotions.
- Night means nightlife businesses, bars, clubs, events, and late experiences.
- Day/Night is product discovery context, not merely system light/dark theme.

## Usage
- Gold is for primary actions, active icons, highlights, and financial emphasis.
- Emerald is a secondary premium accent.
- Black and dark grays anchor the exclusive night experience.
- Ivory and warm white support daytime discovery.

## Components
- Buttons use the shared Ciervo button system.
- Inputs use filled surfaces, gold focus states, and clear error states.
- Cards use consistent radii, surfaces, and restrained shadows.
- Chips use the global chip theme.
- Bottom navigation uses glass/surface treatment with gold active state.

## Rules
- Do not introduce one-off color literals for branding.
- Do not reintroduce orange as the primary brand color.
- Components must adapt through tokens and theme values.
- Keep UI consistent across Auth, Home, Discovery, Wallet, Profile, Place Detail, and Vakupli.
